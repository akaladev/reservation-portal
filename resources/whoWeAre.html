Who we are

The Dome Experience is an exciting and interactive experience designed to get you and yoru family up and learning! We have focused all of our programs on ensuring family fun, collaboration and learning. No matter what activity you take part of, you will have to work toegether to complete the task at hand and earn something new. The Dome Experience is great for famlies of all shapes and sizes and can accomodate up to five people at a time. Each family is given their own personal 30 minute slot to enjoy the experience and valuable family time. The Dome Experience can also be accomodated for school groups.
